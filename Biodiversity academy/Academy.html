Hello World, welcome to the Biodiversity Online Academy, the perfect place to explore and learn new things about the natural world. Our platform is dedicated to spreading awareness about the importance of biodiversity and providing opportunities for everyone to contribute towards its preservation.

Vision

The leading Online academy advocating for biodiversity conservation.

Our Mission

To facilitate the transmission of biodiversity knowledge by providing online training and resources, and encourage the sustainable use of natural resources to meet the demands of the current generation without compromising for the needs of future generations.
